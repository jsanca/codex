Codex was born from two questions:

1. If I were to build a CMS from scratch in modern Java, using Java 25, strong modularity, events, clear APIs, and architectural discipline, what would it look like?

2. If that CMS were designed for an agentic world from the beginning, where AI agents can discover, index, summarize, operate, and explain business content, what would it become?

It would stop being only a CMS.

It would become a semantic business content system:
a platform for modeling, versioning, projecting, searching, and explaining knowledge units across human interfaces and agentic systems.
